About

I am a Ph.D. student in Industrial Engineering at Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology (UNIST), working in the Financial Engineering Lab under the supervision of Prof. Yongjae Lee.

My research focuses on bridging machine learning and mathematical optimization for financial decision-making.

Before starting my Ph.D., I received my Bachelor's degree in Financial Engineering from Ajou University.

Research Interests

  • Machine Learning in Optimization
  • Decision-Focused Learning
  • Portfolio Optimization
  • LLMs & Agents in Finance
